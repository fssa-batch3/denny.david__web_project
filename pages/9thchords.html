9th Chords

In music theory, a ninth chord is a chord that encompasses the interval of a ninth when arranged in close position with the root in the bass. The ninth chord and its inversions exist today, or at least they can exist.Theoretically ninth chords are built with 5 notes called root (1), major third (3), perfect fifth (5), minor seventh (b7) and ninth (9)
